# Content Quality Standard

This encyclopedia uses source-backed facts only.

## Required Rule

- Every product claim must link to an official source, a dated release note, a pricing/help page, or a named local-private evidence file.
- Official sources are recorded as covered only when the actual fact has been extracted into the service page.
- Official sources are recorded as indexed when the source is known but not fully extracted yet.
- Missing official documentation is recorded as missing or needs-recheck.
- Non-official news, X posts, blogs, and community posts are signals. They cannot establish product facts.

## Blocked Content

- Generic praise.
- Unsupported comparisons.
- Pricing or limit claims without a dated official source.
- Private tokens, account identifiers, raw logs, and OAuth secrets.
