For more information, please refer to our article: Retiring GPT-4o and other ChatGPT models ChatGPT Plus is a subscription plan that provides enhanced access to the ChatGPT web app for $20/month. Benefits of ChatGPT Plus Priority access during high-traffic periods : Fewer interruptions even when demand is high. Access to higher GPT-5.5 limits : Use OpenAI’s most advanced model, capable of understanding text, images, and audio. Access to advanced reasoning models: Use models that think through complex problems before answering—delivering stronger performance in coding, planning, and multi-step analysis Faster response speeds : Enjoy quicker replies in conversations. Expanded features : Voice conversations Image generation File uploads and analysis Deep Research tools (where available) Custom GPT creation and use Subscription Details Price : $20/month (billed monthly). Platform : ChatGPT Plus is available on chat.openai.com . Not included : API usage is separate and billed independently. See API pricing . How do I sign up? Log into ChatGPT Click on your profile icon -> Upgrade Plan Select &#x27;Get Plus&#x27; Usage Limits To ensure a smooth experience for all users, Plus subscriptions may include usage limits such as message caps, especially during high demand. These limits may vary based on system conditions. Billing and Invoices You can manage your subscription, view invoices, and cancel your plan by: Log into ChatGPT Click on your profile icon -> Settings Select Account Confirm that you&#x27;re logged in with the same email address or single sign-on provider that was used to purchase your Plus or Pro subscription. Troubleshooting: Charged but still on Free plan If you were charged for ChatGPT Plus or Pro but your account still shows the Free plan, try the steps below: Verify you are signed in with the same authentication method (Google, Apple, Microsoft, or email/password) that you used to subscribe. If you used Sign in with Apple and selected &quot;Hide My Email,&quot; locate the private relay email address in your Apple ID settings and use that to sign in. On the iOS or Android app, go to Settings > Restore purchases to sync your subscription. Log out of ChatGPT on all devices, then log back in using the correct account. If the issue persists, contact us and include the receipt or the last four digits of the card that was charged so we can locate your subscription. Canceling your subscription If you would like to cancel your subscription, please refer to our article: How do I cancel my subscription for ChatGPT Plus or ChatGPT Pro? Refunds If you believe you were incorrectly charged or would like to request a refund, you can request a refund. Refunds are typically processed within 5–10 business days after approval. Please ensure you&#x27;re signed in with the same email address you used to purchase Plus or Pro when submitting your request.
